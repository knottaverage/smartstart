About Smart Start Tutoring

Your child is an individual. Before we recommend a specific tutor or tutoring program for your child, we will confer with you and decide on the best strategy and method to assist your child.

Each student will have a profile that is designed to consider his/her unique learning style, abilities and specific needs.

Together we will agree on goals and objectives for your child. Smart Start Tutoring will provide individual attention to your child in a one-on-one tutoring session.

Your child’s academic needs will be addressed in a positive, constructive manner with success as the main focus. This achievement will help to improve and enhance your child’s self esteem and academic outlook.
